About Mathoria

Mathoria is more than a club — it's a vibrant community of thinkers, solvers, and creators from all departments of RGCET. With a unique blend of logic and fun, we’re redefining how students see and experience mathematics!

🎯 Our Mission

To inspire curiosity, foster analytical thinking, and make mathematics exciting for every student.

📌 What We Do

We conduct workshops, host thrilling competitions, organize puzzle battles, and bring in experts to interact with students.

🚀 Why Join?

Meet amazing minds, challenge your skills, earn recognition, and most importantly — enjoy math like never before!
